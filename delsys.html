Description

Test Drive is a game I created at Delsys, Inc to show off the capabilities of their EMG sensors when used alongside technology such as the Oculus Quest and VR Hand tracking. Through the creation this game, I was able to not only create an enjoyable sensor testing game, but also provided toolkits and prefabs for other sensor developers to use in the future.

Test Drive itself utilizes Oculus Hand Tracking and the EMG functionality of Delsys sensors to create an immersive and controllerless VR driving experience. You are able to physically grip the VR controllers by grasping your hand where the wheel should be and accelerate and brake simply by pushing your foot into the floor to activate the emg. This allows you to travel around without intrustive controllers. I believe that experiences like this are just a sneak peek into what the future of VR may have to offer.
